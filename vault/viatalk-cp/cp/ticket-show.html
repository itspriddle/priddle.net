My adapter is a Linksys PAP2T and I have not changed any settings recently.

Mike Thompson (ViaTalk Support) — Jan 5, 2009 at 4:12 PM

Hi John,

Thank you for contacting ViaTalk support. I've checked your account and can see that your device is currently registered on our network.

Could you please try the following steps:

1. Unplug the power from your PAP2T for 30 seconds
2. Plug it back in and wait for the lights to stabilize
3. Try dialing *73 to reset your call forwarding
4. Then try making an outbound call

If the issue persists, please let us know what lights are showing on your adapter and whether you hear a dial tone when you pick up the phone.

Best regards,
Mike Thompson
ViaTalk Technical Support
