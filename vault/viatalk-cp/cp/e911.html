E911

Enhanced 911 (E911) service ensures that when you dial 911 from your ViaTalk phone, emergency services are dispatched to your registered address. It is extremely important that you keep this address up to date. If you move or change locations, update your E911 address immediately.

Important: Your E911 address must be your physical location where the ViaTalk phone adapter is installed. P.O. Boxes are not accepted.
