Anonymous Call Block

Anonymous Call Block (ACB) determines how calls from blocked or anonymous numbers are handled. Choose one of the options below to control what happens when someone calls you with a hidden caller ID.

  • Disabled - Anonymous calls will ring through normally.
  • Send to Voicemail - Anonymous calls are sent directly to your voicemail box.
  • Authenticate Caller - The caller will be asked to state their name before the call rings through. You will hear their name and can choose to accept or reject the call.
  • Play Busy Signal - Anonymous callers will hear a busy signal and be unable to leave a message.
  • Block Call - Anonymous calls are completely blocked. The caller hears a message stating that anonymous calls are not accepted.
