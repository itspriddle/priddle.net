How to Set Up Call Forwarding

Category:
Features & Settings
Author:
jpriddle
Created:
Dec 10, 2008
Last Updated:
Jan 15, 2009

Call forwarding allows you to redirect incoming calls to another phone number. This is useful when you are away from home or want calls to ring on your cell phone instead of your ViaTalk line.

To enable call forwarding, log in to your ViaTalk control panel at cp.viatalk.com. Navigate to the "Features" section in the left sidebar and click on "Call Forwarding." Enter the phone number you want calls forwarded to in the destination field. You can choose between "Always Forward" to redirect all calls, or "Forward When Busy" to only redirect calls when your line is in use. Click "Save" to activate the feature.

You can also enable call forwarding directly from your phone by dialing *72 followed by the destination number. Wait for the confirmation tone, then hang up. To disable call forwarding from your phone, dial *73 and wait for the confirmation tone. Note that call forwarding from the phone will override any settings in the control panel.

If you experience any issues with call forwarding, make sure the destination number includes the area code and that it is a valid, working number. International forwarding is not supported on residential plans. Business plan customers can contact support to enable international call forwarding for an additional fee.
