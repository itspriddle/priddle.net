Childs Automotive is a full service automotive repair shop in Saratoga Springs, New York. We specialize in Honda, Acura, and Toyota repairs, but thanks to our top notch technicians we now offer service on domestic vehicles too. From simple oil changes and maintenance, to major engine or transmission repair, Childs Automotive has the technology and the experience to get your car or truck back on the road.
