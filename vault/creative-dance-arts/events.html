Events
December 15, 2015
Winter Recital 2015

Join us for our annual Winter Recital at the Clifton Park-Halfmoon Library Community Room. Doors open at 6:00 PM. All classes will be performing. Family and friends are welcome!

May 15–16, 2015
Spring Showcase

Our Spring Showcase at the Clifton Common features performances from all of our classes. This is a wonderful opportunity for our dancers to perform in an outdoor venue and share their hard work with the community.

June 20, 2015
Year End Performance

The highlight of our dance year! Our Year End Performance showcases the progress and achievements of all our students. This is a ticketed event held at a local theater venue.

October 31, 2015
Halloween Dance Party

Come in costume for a fun-filled afternoon of dancing and treats! Open to all current students and their siblings. No registration required.

Ongoing
Community Performances

Throughout the year, our students participate in various charitable and community performances. These events give our dancers real-world performance experience while giving back to the community.
