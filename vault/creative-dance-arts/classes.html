Classes
Creative Movement (Ages 3–4)

An introduction to dance for our youngest students. Creative Movement classes use imaginative play, music, and basic dance steps to develop coordination, rhythm, and a love of movement. Classes are 30 minutes long.

 
Pre-Ballet/Tap Combo (Ages 4–6)

This combination class introduces young dancers to the fundamentals of both ballet and tap. Students learn basic positions, simple choreography, and musicality in a fun and structured environment. Classes are 45 minutes long.

 
Ballet (Ages 6+)

Our ballet program offers progressive levels of classical ballet training. Students develop proper technique, flexibility, strength, and grace. Classes emphasize correct alignment and build a strong foundation for all forms of dance.

 
Tap (Ages 6+)

Tap classes focus on rhythm, timing, and footwork. Students learn a variety of tap styles from Broadway to rhythm tap. Classes are offered at multiple levels from beginner through advanced.

 
Jazz (Ages 7+)

Jazz classes combine high energy choreography with technical training. Students develop strength, flexibility, and performance skills through contemporary and classic jazz styles.

 
Adult Classes

It’s never too late to dance! Our adult classes are designed for dancers of all experience levels. Whether you’re returning to dance after years away or trying it for the first time, our adult classes provide a welcoming and fun environment.
