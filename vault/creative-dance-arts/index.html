December 15, 2015 Winter Recital 2015

Join us for our annual Winter Recital! All classes will be performing at the Clifton Park-Halfmoon Library Community Room. Doors open at 6:00 PM. See your class schedule for specific performance times.

Registration for our Fall 2015 season is now open! We have classes available for all ages and skill levels. Visit our Classes page or contact us for more information about scheduling and availability.

Thank you to everyone who came out to support our dancers at this year’s end-of-year performance. The students did an amazing job and we couldn’t be more proud. Special thanks to all the parents and volunteers who made it possible.
