Faculty
Patti Friedman
Artistic Director / Owner

Patti Friedman founded Creative Dance Arts in 1976 with a vision of creating a non-competitive dance school that focuses on the joy of dance. With over 40 years of teaching experience, Patti has trained thousands of students in ballet, tap, jazz, and creative movement.

Patti holds certifications from Dance Masters of America and continues to attend workshops and conventions to bring the latest techniques to her students.

Email Patti Friedman at dance@creativedancearts.org
 
Laurie Fitsik
Information Coordinator

Laurie has been with Creative Dance Arts for over 15 years, serving as the primary point of contact for families and managing the day-to-day operations of the studio. She ensures that everything runs smoothly so that students and teachers can focus on what they do best — dance.

Email Laurie Fitsik at info@creativedancearts.org
 
Assistant Instructor
Dance Instructor

Our assistant instructors are experienced dancers who bring passion and expertise to every class. Many are former Creative Dance Arts students who have gone on to study dance at the collegiate level and returned to share their knowledge with the next generation of dancers.
