Will's Frequently Asked Questions.

1.) Do I need a cell phone to sell my vehicle with the text message feature and for sale signs?
No, a cell phone is not required. You will be notified of all text message inquiries on your item for sale by text message and/or email.

2.) How do I set up the text message feature to work with item I am selling?
In order for the text message feature to work, you must "activate" your Will Dikker for sale signs. It's easy and takes only a minute or two. Simply log into your account and follow the prompts.

3.) Are the Will Dikker for sale signs included?
Our Enhanced and Ultimate packages both include three Will Dikker for sale signs. *Basic package does not include for sale signs or text messaging.
