Text Message Info

What are Will Dikker Text Messages?
Will Dikker text messages are SMS messages that contain Will Dikker classifieds listing information as well as promotional information the end-user has signed up for.


How do I sign up to receive Will Dikker text messages?
End-users can sign up to receive Will Dikker text messages by entering their mobile number on the WillDikker.com website, by texting in a Will Dikker text code or keyword to the shortcode 78501, or by agreeing to receive future Will Dikker text messages after entering in a sweepstakes. You will only receive Will Dikker text messages for items that you have signed up for. Will Dikker text messages are not spam and are not mobile spam.


Is it free?
Although all text messages sent by Will Dikker are complimentary, standard text messaging rates do apply. Depending on your text plan, you may be charged by your carrier.


What if I don't want to receive any more promotional messages?
To stop receiving any Will Dikker text messages, simply text "STOP" to the shortcode 78501. After doing so you will no longer receive any Will Dikker text messages.


What if I want back in?
You can start receiving Will Dikker text messages again by following any of the steps outlined above section: "How do I sign up to receive Will Dikker text messages". You may also email our support staff at support@willdikker.com. They will contact you to confirm that you would like to re-enable your mobile device.


What if I want more info?
To request more info simply text "HELP" to the shortcode 78501.


Commands
STOP: At any time users can text "STOP" to the shortcode 78501. This will prevent them from receiving any future Will Dikker text messages.

HELP: At any time users can text "HELP" to the shortcode 78501. Texting "HELP" will return the following message: For more info: go to www.willdikker.com or email: support@willdikker.com. Text STOP to stop. Standard rates apply.
