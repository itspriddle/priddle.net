Will's Frequently Asked Questions.

1.) Do I need a cell phone to sell my vehicle with the text message feature and for sale signs?
No, a cell phone is not required. You will be notified of all text message inquiries on your item for sale by text message and/or email. This means, if you do not have a cell phone, you will simply receive email notifications and correspondence only and not text messages.

2.) How do I set up the text message feature to work with item I am selling?
In order for the text message feature to work, you must "activate" your Will Dikker for sale signs. It's easy and takes only a minute or two. Simply log into your account and follow the prompts.

3.) How do I know my for sale signs are "activated" and the text message feature is working?
After you have activated your for sale signs on WillDikker.com, simply try out the text message feature for yourself by texting the unique text code shown on your for sale signs to 78501. If your for sale signs have been successfully activated, you will receive the message that potential buyers will receive when then send a text message for information.

4.) Do I have to pay more when someone sends a text message requesting info about my item for sale?
No, WillDikker.com never charges for individual text messages. There are no "per-message" charges or hidden fees of any kind to you or to potential buyers. Standard message rates from your carrier, however, may apply.

5.) Are the Will Dikker for sale signs included?
Our Enhanced and Ultimate packages both include three Will Dikker for sale signs (2- 7"x11" and 1- 9"x15"). Additional for sale signs and larger sizes are also available for purchase. *Basic package does not include for sale signs or text messaging.

6.) When will I receive my Will Dikker for sale signs?
Processing and delivery is fast and FREE! Once your account and/or your listing have been created, your Will Dikker for sale signs will be shipped out no later than the following business day via USPS Priority Mail.

7.) Are Will Dikker for sale signs safe for my vehicle?
Yes, they are safe? Our for sale signs are made from a high quality removable-adhesive-backed vinyl material. The material has been time-tested and proven to be safe after more than six years of use on tens of thousands of vehicles throughout the country.

8.) Is it difficult to put on or take off the Will Dikker for sale signs?
No, application and removal are both very easy and fast. To apply, all you will need is a squeegee or a credit card, glass cleaner or a squirt bottle with soapy water and a clean towel. To remove, simply grab an edge and carefully peel away from surface.

9.) How long will the for sale signs last?
Will Dikker For Sale Signs are made from the very best materials and inks available and can literally last for years. If applied per recommendation, they will not fade or peel in the time it takes to sell your item.

10.) Does Will Dikker have a referral or affiliate program?
Yes, we do! Please contact us if you are interested in earning ongoing recurring revenue by promoting WillDikker.com
